About Me

My name is Harold Doran and I have been working in measurement for 20 years. I have always had a passion for developing tools that statisticians, psychometricians, and data scientists can use to make their work more efficient. It's driven mainly by my own curiosity to explore psychometric and statistical techniques at their core to make sure that I understand them better myself. I often find that the detailed process of writing code reveals much about the methods we routinely use.
